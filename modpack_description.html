⚔️ COLONYCRAFT ⚔️

Colony Survival × Minecraft

Build. Defend. Conquer.
ColonyCraft brings the full Colony Survival experience into Minecraft. Manage your own medieval colony with 27+ specialized jobs, a dynamic quest system, nightly monster wave raids, and colonists that feel truly alive.

🏰 Core Features

🏴 Colony Banner

Place it to found your colony and attract colonists from the wilderness

👷 27+ Jobs

Farmer, Miner, Blacksmith, Guard, Builder, Researcher, Shepherd, Baker, and many more

📜 16 Quests

Guided progression from your first banner to a thriving medieval city

⚔️ Wave Raids

Defend your colony from increasingly dangerous nightly monster attacks

🔬 Research Tree

Unlock new jobs and technologies by spending Science Points

📦 Stockpile

540-slot centralized storage with pagination — colonists deposit resources automatically

💬 Living Colonists

Named NPCs with speech bubbles, status indicators, and unique job textures

🏗️ Builder & Digger

Assign colonists to construct buildings or excavate terrain for you

🚀 Getting Started

1   Place a Colony Banner to claim your territory

2   Build a Stockpile near the banner for centralized storage

3   Place Job Blocks (Farmer Hut, Miner Hut, Guard Tower…)

4   Colonists arrive automatically and claim available jobs

5   Use the Survey Wand to assign work areas

6   Complete Quests to progress and unlock new jobs

7   Defend your colony from nightly raids!
