⚔️ ColonyCraft Modpack ⚔️

Colony Survival meets Minecraft

🏰 What is ColonyCraft?

ColonyCraft brings the full Colony Survival experience into Minecraft! Build and manage your own medieval colony with 27+ specialized jobs, a dynamic quest system, nightly monster wave raids, and colonists that feel truly alive.

🎮 Modpack Contents

🏗️ColonyCraftThe core colony management mod
⚡Fabric APIRequired library
🚀SodiumMassive FPS boost
💎LithiumServer-side optimization
🌅Iris ShadersShader support for the included shader pack
📋Mod MenuIn-game mod configuration

⭐ Key Features

  • 🏰 Colony Banner — Place it to start your colony and attract colonists
  • 👷 27+ Jobs — Farmer, Miner, Blacksmith, Guard, Builder, Researcher, and many more
  • 📜 16 Quests — Guided progression from first banner to thriving city
  • ⚔️ Wave Raids — Defend your colony from nightly monster attacks
  • 🔬 Research System — Unlock new jobs and technologies with Science Points
  • 📦 Stockpile System — 540-slot centralized storage with pagination
  • 💬 Living Colonists — Named NPCs with speech bubbles, status indicators, and personalities
  • 🐑 Animal Husbandry — Shepherds herd sheep, Cow Herders tend cattle
  • 🏗️ Builder & Digger — Colonists that construct and excavate for you
  • 🎨 Custom Textures — Unique villager-faced textures for every job
  • 🌄 Custom Shader Pack — Included warm Colony Survival-style shaders (Iris compatible)
  • 🎮 Custom Title Screen — Animated cinematic main menu with particles

🛠️ How to Play

  1. Place a Colony Banner to claim your territory
  2. Build a Stockpile near the banner for centralized storage
  3. Place Job Blocks (Farmer Hut, Miner Hut, etc.) to create jobs
  4. Colonists arrive automatically and claim available jobs
  5. Use the Survey Wand to assign work areas
  6. Complete Quests to progress and unlock new jobs
  7. Defend your colony from nightly raids!

📋 Requirements

  • Minecraft 1.21.11
  • Fabric Loader 0.19.1+
  • Java 21

🎨 Shader Pack

This modpack includes the ColonyCraft Shaders — a warm, cozy shader pack designed to match the Colony Survival aesthetic. Features soft shadows, warm color grading, cinematic vignette, and day/night tinting. Enable it via Options → Video → Shader Packs.
