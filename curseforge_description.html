🏰 ColonyCraft — Colony Survival in Minecraft

What if Colony Survival and Minecraft had a baby?

ColonyCraft is a Fabric mod that recreates the full Colony Survival gameplay loop — colonists with jobs, nightly monster waves, food economy, and colony management — all inside vanilla Minecraft.

✨ Features

🏰 Colony Management

  • Place a Colony Banner to found your colony
  • Press ; to open Colony Management screen
  • Track food, science, population and days survived
  • Colony HUD shows real-time status on screen
  • Recruit colonists directly from the management screen
  • Chunk loading — base terrain stays loaded around your banner

👷 27+ Colonist Jobs

🌲 GatheringWoodcutter, Forester, Miner, Farmer, Berry Farmer, Fisherman, Water Gatherer
🔥 ProcessingCook, Smelter, Blacksmith, Tanner, Tailor, Fletcher, Stonemason, Composter, Grinder, Potter, Glassblower
🔭 ScienceResearcher, Alchemist
🐝 AnimalsBeekeeper, Chicken Farmer, Shepherd, Cow Herder
🏗 ConstructionBuilder, Digger
🏹 DefenseGuard (Bow), Guard (Crossbow), Guard (Musket)

💬 Living Colony

  • Colonists chat with each other via speech bubbles
  • Problem alerts appear above their heads when hungry, homeless, or missing supplies
  • Dynamic status display — see exactly what each colonist is doing (🌾 Harvesting, ⛏ Mining, 🍖 Eating, ☾ Sleeping...)

⚔️ Nightly Monster Waves

  • Waves spawn at dusk every in-game day
  • Dusk warning + imminent warning + wave countdown HUD timer
  • Banner Raiders — enemies that pathfind to destroy your Colony Banner
  • Wave size scales with colonist count and days survived

📜 Quest System

  • 16 progression quests guiding you from first banner to master colony
  • Tutorial → Survival → Growth → Knowledge quest chains
  • Science point rewards for completing quests

🔨 Chisel Tool

  • Right-click blocks to cycle through decorative variants
  • Supports 20+ block families: Stone, Deepslate, Sandstone, Quartz, and more

📖 In-Game Guidebook

  • 10-page illustrated guide with book-style GUI
  • Covers all gameplay mechanics

🚀 Getting Started

  1. Craft a Colony Banner and place it on flat ground
  2. A Stockpile and food are given as a starter kit
  3. Press ; to open Colony Management
  4. Place job blocks within 32 blocks of the banner
  5. Use the Colony Survey Wand to mark work areas
  6. Colonists spawn automatically and start working
  7. Survive the nightly wave at dusk!

📦 Requirements

  • Minecraft 1.21.11
  • Fabric Loader ≥ 0.19.1
  • Fabric API (required)
  • Java 21

🎨 Recommended

  • ColonyCraft Shaders — included lightweight Iris shader pack with warm Colony Survival-style lighting and soft shadows
  • Iris Shaders — needed to use the shader pack
